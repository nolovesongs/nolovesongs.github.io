ABOUT NO LOVE SONGS

SOUTH BROOKLYN GRUNGE TRIO.

NO LOVE SONGS is the creation of RYAN BAZINET, who leads the group on guitar and vocals, with GUSTAVO MARRON on drums and ERNESTO "LUI" RODRIGUEZ on bass. The band's heavy sound makes them equally at home in the environs of punk, hardcore, metal, or straight up rock.

In 2024 the band founded FRIENDS & ALIENS RECORDS.
